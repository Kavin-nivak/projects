The attack on titans

BLEACH

Japanese anime "Attack On Titan" ("Shingeki no Kyojin") is based Hajime Isayama's
bestselling original manga, which ran from 2009 to 2021.The anime was produced by
Wit Studio up through Season 3, with Season 4(the final season, which debuted in
three parts) produced by the animation studio MAPPA.The show is known as one of
the best anime for newcomers due its mostly linear, but still shocking,storyline.

High school student Ichigo Kurosaki, who has the ability
see ghosts, gains soul reaper powers from Rukia Kuchiki
and sets out to save the world from "Hollows".
